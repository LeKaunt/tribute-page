Raging Rebels

3 1/2 people with a dream

Here's a timeline of the category 6 hurricane:

  • December 25th, 2006- 2 of the original members receive a PC for christmas
  • January 12th 2007 - Internet is introduced into the household
  • January 13th 2007 - Watching youtube videos, become a hobby
  • March 21st 2007 - The 2 members recruit a third, and start talking about making videos
  • April 12th 2007 - The 3 members receive a camcoder
  • May 14th 2007 - Production has begun
  • May 29th 2007 - First video in the Raging Rebels Youtube channel has been published
  • June 10th 2007 - During summer vacation, the three members decide to make numerous videos
  • September 3rd 2008 - Due to social media and excellent marketing, a number of classmates heard of the Raging Rebels
  • July 4th 2009 - The members forced to move locations due to the economic recession. Leaving behind everything
  • August 29th 2009 - Starting a new school, knowing no one. The members stopped production completely
